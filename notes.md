1、游戏背景 Background
为了吸收人类的负能量、完成恶魔学院的考核，胡主任伪装身份，潜入职场。短短几年，他便从一位风华正茂的男狐，熬成了生无可恋的濒死打工人。
如今，他将恶魔之力带到了你的公司——同事变得阴阳怪气，领导开始令人窒息。整个职场，正在被一步步黑化。
你能扛住这场来自“打工恶魔”的终极考验，还是彻底坠入加班地狱？
To absorb humanity’s negative energy and complete his assessment at the Demon Academy, Mr.Fox went undercover and infiltrated the workplace. In just a few short years, he went from being a handsome, charismatic male fox to a burnt-out, near-death office worker.
Now, he brings his demonic powers to your company—your colleagues have become snarky and two-faced, and your boss is suffocating to deal with. The entire workplace is slowly being corrupted.
Can you withstand this ultimate trial from the “Workplace Demon,” or will you fall completely into overtime hell?

2、玩法说明 How to Play
获胜条件：在每轮倒计时结束前做出角色要求的正确手势；每答对一次工作量 -1，当工作量降到 0 时游戏胜利。
失败条件：若在倒计时内手势错误或超时未作答，则压力值 +1；当压力值达到 10 时游戏失败。
手势含义：
👍🏻：领导您说的太对了！
👎🏻：你快闭嘴别说了！
🖐🏻：Sorry我要去洗手间，下次再聊！

Win Condition: Before the countdown ends in each round, perform the correct gesture required by your role. Each correct answer reduces your Workload by 1. When Workload reaches 0, you win the game.

Lose Condition: If you make the wrong gesture or fail to answer before the countdown ends, your Stress Level increases by 2. When Stress Level reaches 10, you lose the game.

Gesture Meanings:
👍🏻: “You’re absolutely right, boss!”
👎🏻: “Would you please just shut up already?!”
🖐🏻: “Excuse me, I need to go to the restroom. Talk next time!”
